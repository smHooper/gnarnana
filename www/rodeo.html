Whitewater Rodeo

Get ready to throw down on the Backyard Play Wave! Participants will have two 45-second runs on the wave and one on the surf wave rock, water-level permitting. Judges will score surfs on wave entry, stabilization, movement, and steeeeez. Kayaks, packrafts, and spectators welcome!

When

Friday afternoon, July 10th (exact time TDB)

Logistics

Meet at the public boat launch on the Nenana @ Denali Park Village, 231 Parks Highway. A shuttle will be available to the the Grassy Knoll put-in (Mile Marker 222 Parks Highway) for particants. Spectators will have to arrange their own shuttle (for insurance reasons–sorry!).

The Play Wave is a 5-mile paddle from the put-in. Spectators will be asked to leave the surf wave open for safety and participants. The take-out at Denali Park Village is a 6-mile paddle from the Play Wave with one Class III rapid. See the River Info section for more info.
